A novel workflow solution


Simple is a cloud-based field service management solution that streamlines internal business processes through both a mobile app and desktop dashboard.

We support a wide range of field service industries with interchangeable tools such as an integrated CRM, real-time scheduling and dispatching, expense tracking, invoice creation and billing, and technician client reminders.



Why Simple?


Short answer, because it’s simple. Very.

With its straightforward interface and uncomplicated approach to workflow management, Simple drastically reduces the time it takes dispatchers to assign and manage workload and maintain organized, up-to-date accounting – all with minimal clicks.

Dispatchers communicate with technicians through the app via a traditional phone text messaging system, the tried-and-true method of communication preferred by 91% of technicians. Unlike other field service software solutions, Simple understands that technicians do not want to deal with complicated apps and a steep learning curve. With our app, they maintain their longtime, comfortable routine, automatically processing the vast majority of their jobs with our automated text messaging system (direct messaging with the dispatcher is also available).



Problem Solved.


Forget inefficient workflows and overly-complicated apps. Simple is here to streamline your business, dramatically ramp up your productivity, and promote your company's growth all while maintaining the familiarity of text messaging dispatcher-to-technician communication.
